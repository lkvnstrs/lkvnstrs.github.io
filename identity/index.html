Testing the identity bot

We are testing a chat bot experience and we need to understand how well it can capture information from people.

Please answer the questions that the bot asks to the best of your ability. It will ask for your name, email address, and company name - we will NOT be using any of the information that we collect, so you can use either your own information or make something up.

At the end of the bot, you will receive a code to receive credit for interacting.
